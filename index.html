About 匠の豚

匠の豚 (Takumi no Buta) brings premium-level graded pork to the Canadian market. Our packaging design stands out with a distinctive style inspired by research findings to differentiate from generic labels. The label also features pork grading information to align it with beef, emphasizing quality and craftsmanship.

Discover premium cuts like pork belly and pork cheek, celebrated for their flavor and tenderness. Each cut is prepared with Japanese-inspired methods to bring out its unique qualities.

Recipes & Cuts

Explore the unique flavors and versatility of 匠の豚. From traditional recipes to premium cuts, here’s a closer look:

Tonkatsu

A famous Japanese dish of deep-fried pork cutlet with a thick, fruity brown sauce. Tonkatsu is a type of yoshoku (Western-style food).

Horumon-yaki

A specialty of Osaka, made by grilling marinated offal meat over a charcoal or gas flame.

Kakuni

A braised pork belly dish simmered in a sweet-savory sauce, inspired by Chinese braised pork belly.

Yakiton

Bite-sized pieces of pork skewered and grilled, including cuts like loin and belly.

Buta-Maki

Pork wrapped around or with ingredients, such as bacon-wrapped cherry tomatoes or pork rolled with shiso.

Tonkotsu Ramen

A creamy pork bone soup from Hakata, Kyushu, served with thin noodles, green onions, and char siu.
